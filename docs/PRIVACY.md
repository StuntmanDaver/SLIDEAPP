# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-01-14

## 1. Introduction
Slide ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and services.

## 2. Information We Collect
- **Account Information:** Email address, name (optional).
- **Payment Information:** Processed securely by Stripe. We do not store full card numbers.
- **Usage Data:** Scan history, pass usage, and app interaction logs.
- **Device Information:** Device ID, OS version (for fraud prevention and support).

## 3. How We Use Your Information
- To provide the Service (issuing and redeeming passes).
- To process payments and manage subscriptions.
- To prevent fraud and abuse.
- To communicate with you regarding your account.

## 4. Data Sharing
We do not sell your data. We share data only with:
- **Service Providers:** Stripe (payments), Supabase (hosting).
- **Venue Partners:** Validating your pass at the door (scan result only).
- **Legal Authorities:** If required by law.

## 5. Your Rights
You can request account deletion at any time via the App settings or by contacting support.

## 6. Contact Us
For privacy questions, contact: privacy@slide.app
